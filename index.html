About Us

Building the backbone of Indian robotics

Based in Salem, Tamil Nadu, Elamani Tech pioneers affordable, locally manufactured robotic systems tailored for India's unique industrial, agricultural, and environmental conditions.

Incorporated on 15 July 2024, we are positioned to capture the growing demand for automation across manufacturing, agriculture, and defence sectors—leveraging Tamil Nadu's emerging industrial corridor and proximity to engineering institutions like Sona College.

The Challenge We're Solving

Foreign Dependency

Not Built for India

Global robotic systems aren't designed for India's unique industrial, agricultural, and environmental conditions.
